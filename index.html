Robotic Claw
Team Member January 2019

For my APSC 101 class, my team and I got to build a robotic claw using aluminum sheets, pop rivets, hobby sonar sensors, and Arduino software. The claw was designed and built over a month of work, and in the end it was capable of picking up 1-5Kg blocks, large scoops of pasta, and a really long stick. The claw uses the sonar sensor to detect when to open, and then releases again when it detects a bin to drop the contents into.
